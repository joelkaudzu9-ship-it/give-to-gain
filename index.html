What We're Building Together

Two events. One mission. Countless lives touched.

Premed Welcome Function

Before the first lecture, before the first textbook, there's a moment where a student decides: "Am I alone here, or do I have a family?"

The Premed Welcome Function answers that question with food, worship, and faces that become family. For many, it's the first time they feel seen — not as a number, but as a child of God with a calling in healthcare.

Venue Meals Sound Decor Transport

Conversations That Count

Some conversations change everything. The right word at the right time can pull someone back from the edge, ignite a dormant dream, or anchor a faith that's about to drift.

Conversations That Count gathers students, mentors, and speakers to go deep — mental health, identity, purpose, faith and science. The topics textbooks skip but life demands.

Why We Call It Give to Gain

This isn't a transaction. It's a seed.

When you give into this work, you're not funding an event — you're funding an encounter. You're standing in the gap for a student whose life is about to pivot toward purpose.

"Give, and it will be given to you. A good measure, pressed down, shaken together and running over, will be poured into your lap."

— Luke 6:38

We believe that promise still works. As you sow into these students, God is sowing into your own story — your peace, your provision, your place in His plan.

🕊️ Joy — you made someone's first day feel like home
🏥 Legacy — you invested in a healthcare professional who will carry the light into Malawi's hospitals
🌾 Harvest — no seed planted in faith returns empty

Give to Gain. It's not a slogan. It's a kingdom principle.
